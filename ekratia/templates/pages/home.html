What is Ekratia?

Ekratia is a new way of making democracy. Members of Ekratia use the Internet to vote directly for new ideas and laws. In case they are too busy to vote, members can delegate their vote to other members. Members can change their delegates on real time.

Who is behind Ekratia?

Ekratia is being supported and improved by a group of software developers, social scientists, and translators. Ekratia is a fully transparent open source effort. You can see who has done what here.

You can view the platform roadmap and propose ideas using our Trello board.

We are always looking for volunteers. If you want to volunteer, please contact us at @ekratia.
